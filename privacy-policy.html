Detour - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Detour ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Detour mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, username, phone number, date of birth, gender, and authentication credentials (via Clerk).
  • Profile Information: Photos, interests, lifestyle preferences, dating preferences, current location, travel plans, and Instagram handle.
  • Messages: Content of messages you send and receive through the App.
  • Payment Information: Subscription purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. Subscription management is handled via RevenueCat.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your location to show nearby users.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including swipes, matches, and feature usage.
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you notifications about matches and messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your profile to other users.
  • To facilitate matches and messaging between users.
  • To process subscriptions and manage your access to premium features.
  • To send push notifications about matches, messages, and app updates.
  • To improve and optimize the App.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and protect users.

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Other Users: Your profile information (name, photos, interests, location) is visible to other App users.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services including Convex (database), Clerk (authentication), RevenueCat (subscriptions), and Expo (push notifications).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Convex cloud infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your profile information within the App.
  • Update: You can update your profile information through the Edit Profile screen.
  • Delete: You can request account deletion by contacting us. We will delete your account and associated data.
  • Notifications: You can manage push notification preferences through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Subscriptions

Detour offers a subscription service ("Detour+") with a free trial period. Subscription purchases and billing are managed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. You can manage or cancel your subscription through your device's subscription settings. RevenueCat is used to manage entitlements across platforms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
